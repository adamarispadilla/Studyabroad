Campus
During my first week at SNU, I was exploring the campus and got lost a few times. I learned later on that this was a common thing. The campus is extremely huge. After learning to navigate the campus, I started to appreciate the beauty of it.

Skills
My time in South Korea included taking a User Interface Programming class, which significantly enhanced my web design skills. This experience equipped me with a valuable skill set I'll carry forward in my future endeavors.

Classes
Studying at Seoul National University (SNU) has been an intellectually invigorating experience. The classes here are not only academically rigorous but also foster critical thinking and creativity. Thank to UI programming course, I feel like my skills have grown.

Connection
I've formed lasting friendships that transcend borders. The weekends I spent exploring the vibrant streets of Seoul with my friends from Thailand, Turkey, France, and Kazakhstan will always hold a place in my heart.
